# PRD 16: Reporting & Analytics
> Phase: 3 | Priority: P2
> Status: Draft | Date: 2026-03-03

---

## Problem Statement

Product leaders need data-driven visibility into team progress, roadmap health, and strategic alignment. They need reports they can share with leadership without manual compilation.

---

## Goals

1. Built-in reports for common PM metrics
2. Custom report builder
3. Scheduled report delivery
4. Export capabilities

---

## Detailed Requirements

### 1. Built-in Reports
- **Status distribution** — items by status (pie/bar chart)
- **Progress over time** — burndown/burnup chart
- **Activity report** — changes per day/week/user
- **Throughput** — items completed per period
- **Cycle time** — average time from created to done
- **OKR progress** — objective achievement rates
- **Capacity utilization** — demand vs capacity per team

### 2. Custom Reports (Enterprise)
- Drag-and-drop report builder
- Data sources: items, feedback, OKRs, capacity
- Chart types: bar, line, pie, stacked, table
- Filters and date range selectors
- Save and share reports

### 3. Check-Ins
- Recurring status updates (weekly/biweekly)
- Template-based (what's done, what's next, blockers)
- Linked to specific items or objectives
- History viewable per item/workspace

### 4. Export & Delivery
- PDF export of any report
- CSV export of report data
- Scheduled email delivery (weekly/monthly digest)
- Share link for live dashboard

---

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 5+ built-in report types
- [ ] Custom report builder (Enterprise)
- [ ] Check-in workflow
- [ ] PDF and CSV export
- [ ] Scheduled email delivery
